Alumni

Since 1965, JG University has been dedicated to providing quality education and fostering lifelong connections. Our commitment to student success extends beyond graduation. The JG University Alumni Relations supports a vibrant community of over 50,000 alumni worldwide. The JG University Alumni Association (JGUAA) comprised of more than 100 active volunteers, works to connect graduates, celebrate achievements, and contribute to outstanding career success.
